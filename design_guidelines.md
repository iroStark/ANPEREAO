# Design Guidelines for ANPERE Website

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach** - Drawing inspiration from Academia BAI's professional educational institution design, adapted for a telecommunications professional association.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Blue: 220 85% 25% (professional authority)
- Light Blue: 210 70% 85% (trust and accessibility)
- White: 0 0% 100% (clean backgrounds)

**Dark Mode:**
- Dark Blue: 220 60% 15%
- Charcoal: 220 20% 10%
- Light Text: 210 30% 90%

**Accent Colors:**
- Orange: 25 90% 55% (sparingly for CTAs)
- Success Green: 140 50% 45% (confirmations)

### Typography
**Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 600-700 weight
- Body: 400-500 weight
- Small text: 400 weight

**Font Sizes:**
- H1: text-4xl to text-6xl
- H2: text-2xl to text-3xl
- H3: text-xl
- Body: text-base
- Small: text-sm

### Layout System
**Spacing Units:** Consistent use of Tailwind units 2, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16
- Tight spacing: p-2, m-2
- Standard spacing: p-4, m-4, gap-4
- Generous spacing: p-8, m-8, gap-8
- Section spacing: py-12, py-16

### Component Library

**Navigation:**
- Clean horizontal menu with dropdown for "Quem Somos"
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Logo positioned left
- Language selector (PT/EN) positioned right

**Hero Section:**
- Full-width slideshow with professional images
- Overlay text with organization mission
- Gradient overlay: from blue-900/70 to transparent
- CTA buttons with blurred backgrounds when over images

**Content Sections:**
- Card-based layouts for publications and events
- Clean typography hierarchy
- Ample white space
- Professional document icons

**Forms:**
- Contact form with floating labels
- Professional styling consistent with brand
- Clear validation states

**Footer:**
- Contact information prominently displayed
- Social media links (if applicable)
- Copyright and organizational details

### Images
**Hero Slideshow:**
- Professional images of telecommunications equipment, towers, or professional meetings
- Images should convey technology, professionalism, and expertise
- Minimum 1920x1080 resolution
- Dark overlay for text readability

**Content Images:**
- Professional headshots for organizational chart
- Event photography for gallery
- Technical equipment imagery for context
- Document thumbnails for publications

**Image Placement:**
- Hero: Full-width slideshow at top
- About section: Professional team/equipment imagery
- Events: Event photography thumbnails
- Gallery: Grid layout for photo collections

### Animations
**Minimal approach:**
- Subtle fade-ins on scroll
- Smooth hover transitions on cards and buttons
- Slideshow transitions: 5-second intervals with fade effect

### Key Design Principles
1. **Professional Authority:** Clean, structured layouts that convey expertise
2. **Accessibility:** High contrast, readable typography, clear navigation
3. **Information Hierarchy:** Clear distinction between sections and content types
4. **Trust Building:** Consistent branding, professional imagery, transparent information structure
5. **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach with professional appearance across all devices

The design should reflect ANPERE's role as a serious professional telecommunications association while maintaining approachability for members and stakeholders.
